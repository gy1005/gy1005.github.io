Biography

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University, advised by Professor Christina Delimitrou. I am interested in cloud computing, computer architecture and computer systems. My research focuses on improving the performance and resource efficiency in datacenters for modern cloud application architecture patterns, such as microservices.

Before studying at Cornell, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016.
